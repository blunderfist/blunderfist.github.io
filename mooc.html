MOOC

I've listed a few certificates from courses I've taken in the Massive Open Online Course world. I think the affordability and flexibility of some of these online education options are amazing and it really gives anyone an opportunity to learn new skills. I listed the certificates below using a Lightbox, which I did NOT build, but borrowed from W3 Schools' tutorials and tweaked to fit my needs. I understand these certificates do not hold any weight on their own, but they do show an effort to learn, and when they are backed up by projects in a portfolio I think it can help an entry level job candidate show their talent to hiring managers.
